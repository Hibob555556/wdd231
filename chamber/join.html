Non-Profit Membership

The Non-Profit Membership is designed for charitable and service-based organizations that contribute to community well-being. Members at this level gain access to chamber resources, networking opportunities, and visibility to help further their mission while building partnerships with local businesses and leaders.

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership is an excellent entry point for businesses wanting to connect with the local business community. Members gain access to networking opportunities, chamber communications, and resources developed to support business recognition and interaction within the region.

Silver Membership

Silver Members receive enhanced visibility and greater access to chamber programs and events. This level supports business growth through expanded networking, promotional opportunities, and participation in initiatives that strengthen the local economy.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership provides premium access and esteem within the Chamber of Commerce. Members at this level play a leadership role in shaping the business environment by supporting advocacy efforts, economic development initiatives, and community partnerships. Gold Members are recognized as major contributors toward the success and vitality of the local business community.
